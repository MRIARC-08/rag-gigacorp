SECTION 2: RETURN PROCESSES
[Line 32]
Q: What is GigaCorp's return policy?
A: GigaCorp offers a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all 
   products. Items must be unused, in original packaging, 
   and accompanied by proof of purchase.

[Line 37]
Q: How do I initiate a return?
A: To initiate a return:
   1. Log into your GigaCorp account
   2. Go to Orders > Select Order > Request Return
   3. Print the prepaid return label
   4. Drop off at any registered courier partner
   Refund is processed within 5-7 business days of receiving 
   the returned item.

[Line 45]
Q: Are there any non-returnable items?
A: The following items cannot be returned:
   - Digital downloads and software licenses
   - Perishable goods
   - Customized or personalized items
   - Items marked "Final Sale"
   - Opened hygiene products

[Line 52]
Q: What if I receive a damaged item?
A: If your item arrives damaged, contact support within 48 hours
   of delivery with photos. GigaCorp will ship a replacement 
   at no cost or issue a full refund — your choice.
